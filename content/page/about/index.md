# Welcome to GetFOSS.org

GetFOSS.org is a no-nonsense resource for anyone who believes that software should respect its users.

We are tired of the direction the tech industry has taken: forced subscriptions, telemetry masquerading as "features," and operating systems that treat you like a tenant on your own hardware.

This site is our answer.

## Our Philosophy: Free as in Freedom

When we say **Free**, we aren't just talking about the price tag.

We mean **Free as in Freedom**.

Proprietary software treats you as a data point. It locks you into ecosystems, forces updates you didn't ask for, and harvests your usage habits to serve ads.

FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) flips that dynamic.

* You own the code.
* You control the data.
* You decide what runs on your machine.

If a piece of software doesn't respect your privacy, it doesn't belong on your computer. Period.

## What You'll Find Here

We cut through the marketing noise and provide practical, expert-level guides on the best open-source tools available.

Whether you are a seasoned system administrator, a developer, a maker, or a user trying to escape the Windows and macOS ecosystem, we've got you covered.

### Operating Systems

From the rock-solid stability of Debian and Enterprise Linux to the bleeding-edge DIY world of Arch and Gentoo, and the hardened security of privacy-focused distributions.

### Engineering & Maker Tools

CAD, CAM, 3D-printing slicers, and EDA tools that rival proprietary giants without the massive licensing costs.

### Security & Privacy

Tools to audit networks, encrypt data, improve security, and regain control over your online privacy.

### Everyday Productivity

Office suites, password managers, backup tools, and utilities that simply work without unnecessary bloat or telemetry.

## Who Is This For?

This site is for:

* Tinkerers who compile their own kernels.
* Privacy advocates who block trackers at the network level.
* Developers who prefer open standards.
* System administrators running Linux infrastructure.
* Small businesses that refuse to pay endless subscription fees.
* Anyone ready to take back control of their digital life.
