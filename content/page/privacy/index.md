### Our Philosophy

Your privacy is very important to us. At GetFoss ([getfoss.org](https://www.getfoss.org/)), we are committed to gathering as little personal data as possible—ideally, no data at all. When data is necessary, we protect and respect it. We do not view personal data as an asset; we view it as a liability. We are transparent about what we collect, and we never store contact information for marketing purposes.

## Privacy Policy

#### 1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how GetFoss ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website. By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

**Important Note About Our Service:** GetFoss is an informational directory. We do not host, store, distribute, or transmit any software files, programs, or torrents. Our service strictly provides hyperlinks that redirect users to the official websites and legitimate download sources of the respective software developers. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or download mechanisms of these third-party official sources.

#### 2. Collected Data

We build our tools with privacy in mind. However, to ensure the website functions properly and securely, certain data is collected automatically:

*   **Server and Website Information:** Our servers (such as Nginx) automatically record standard networking information provided by your browser when you visit our site. This may include your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, and operating system. This information is stored in secure logs for site maintenance, security, and spam protection.
*   **Analytics:** We use analytics tools to gather anonymized data about page visits. This helps us understand general traffic patterns and see which software projects are the most popular, without tracking you individually.

#### 3. Third-Party Advertising

To help fund our project, we may use third-party advertising networks. We strive to use privacy-respecting ad providers (such as EthicalAds) that do not track users across the web to serve personalized ads.

If an advertising provider uses cookies, your IP address may be temporarily processed by them to serve ads or enforce geographic restrictions, but we do not have access to this data. Please refer to our Cookie Policy below for more details.

#### 4. Your Rights

Most of the data we collect is involuntary and standard server traffic. In accordance with the GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

*   **Access & Portability:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
*   **Correction:** Update or correct your data (if applicable).
*   **Deletion:** Request the deletion of your personal data, except where we are required to retain it for security, legal, or site maintenance purposes.
*   **Object to Processing:** Ask us to stop processing your data.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

#### 5. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information. While no system is completely secure, we are committed to protecting your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

## Third-Party Comment Section (Disqus)

Our website uses the Disqus commenting system to allow user interaction and discussions. Disqus is a third-party service provided by Zeta Global.

By choosing to view or interact with the comment section, please be aware of the following:

*   **Data Collection:** Disqus collects personal data such as IP addresses, browser identifiers, and comment history to function and to serve personalized content and ads.
*   **Advertising:** Because we use the free version of Disqus, it may display advertisements within the comment section.
*   **Cookies:** Disqus sets various cookies on your browser to track interactions and serve ads.

**Our Stance:** We believe in digital freedom and privacy. The decision to use a proprietary, ad-supported comment system was made purely due to financial constraints, as we cannot currently afford a premium, ad-free tier or a custom-built solution. 

**Protecting Yourself:** We strongly encourage our readers to use content blockers (such as uBlock Origin) or privacy extensions (like Privacy Badger) to block Disqus tracking scripts and advertisements if you choose not to interact with the comments. Your digital footprint is your responsibility, and we respect your right to block third-party trackers.

**Do Not Sell My Data:** Under data privacy regulations, you have the right to opt-out of Disqus selling your personal information. You can exercise this right by clicking the "**Do Not Sell My Data**" link located at the bottom of the Disqus comment widget, or by visiting their <span style="color: #e03e2d;">[Data Sharing Settings](https://disqus.com/data-sharing-settings/)</span> page directly.

For more information on how Disqus handles your data, please refer to the <span style="color: #e03e2d;">[Disqus Privacy Policy](https://disqus.com/privacy-policy)</span>.

## Cookie Policy

#### 1. How Does GetFoss Use Cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of text stored on your device that allows a website to recognize your browser. Cookies help us maintain a consistent experience and gather site usage data.

When you use GetFoss, we and our third-party partners may use cookies for the following purposes:

*   **Essential Cookies:** Required for the basic functioning of the website.
*   **Analytics Cookies:** We use Google Analytics (or similar tools) on getfoss.org to measure the number of visits and determine the popularity of software projects. This data is anonymized and aggregated.
*   **Advertising Cookies:** Our ad partners may use cookies to display non-personalized advertisements. We prioritize ad networks that respect user privacy and do not build intrusive tracking profiles.

#### 2. Third-Party Cookies

When you click a link on GetFoss to visit an official software source, that third-party website may set its own cookies. We have no control over these third-party cookies and are not responsible for their use. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of the external sites you visit.

For more information on how third parties use cookies, you can visit:

1. <span style="color: #e03e2d;">[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)</span>
2. <span style="color: #e03e2d;">[Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)</span>

#### 3. How to Restrict or Delete Cookies

You can adjust your internet browser settings to reject some or all cookies, or to notify you when a cookie is being placed. Please note that blocking essential cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

You can also opt out of personalized advertising from various networks by visiting:

1. <span style="color: #e03e2d;">[Your Online Choices (EU)](https://www.youronlinechoices.eu/)</span>
2. <span style="color: #e03e2d;">[Network Advertising Initiative (US)](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)</span>

You can manually delete cookies at any time through your browser settings, though this will not prevent the site from setting new cookies unless you adjust your browser preferences.
